About Us

At Strength and Conditioning Coaches we believe everyone should have the opportunity to enjoy a fit and healthy lifestyle. So we have made it very simple, affordable & convenient for everyone to achieve their personal health goals. Whether you're a beginner or a pro athlete young or old, our professional trainers are here to help everybody in finding the best fitness path that is suitable for them. Our clients are extremely important to us. They are the reason for our existence and what we do.
Sabrina & Lukasz

Meet the Coaches

The gym is an intimidating place for people, just because they maybe haven’t played sport or been active in quite some time. There’s a fear there and people are self-conscious. Alot of my stuff is group-based, and we would do regular nights out, barbecues, get-togethers, and we also did online forums for people to communicate with each other outside class. I want them to feel like they are part of a team, part of something more than just fitness.Coach Sabrina
The most important thing is a sustainable lifestyle change. Do the small changes and stick to them. It will work. It takes a bit longer but it is sustainable. There’s no point dropping a pile of weight fast and then you have a pizza and you’re wearing the pizza the next day.
